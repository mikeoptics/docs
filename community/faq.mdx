Got a question? You're probably not the first to ask it. Below you'll find quick answers to the most common things players ask about The Grind MC — from connecting for the first time to troubleshooting technical issues. If you can't find what you're looking for here, head to **#help-and-support** on Discord and the community will be happy to help.

## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is the server IP?">
    The server IP is `play.thegrindmc.net`. Connect using Minecraft Java Edition — we recommend running the latest stable version for the best experience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the server free to play?">
    Yes, The Grind MC is completely free to play. Donor ranks are entirely optional and exist to support the server's running costs — you'll never need to spend a penny to enjoy the full experience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What Minecraft version does the server support?">
    The server supports the latest stable Java Edition release. Check the **#announcements** channel on Discord for the exact version currently supported, especially after a major Minecraft update.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to apply or register to join?">
    No application or registration is needed. Simply connect to `play.thegrindmc.net` with your Minecraft Java Edition account and you're in.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Rules & Punishments

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where can I read the full rules?">
    You can read the full server rules at [/rules/server-rules](/rules/server-rules) in this guide, or run `/rules` in-game to get a summary at any time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I was banned — what do I do?">
    Join the Discord server and navigate to **#ban-appeals**. Submit an appeal including your in-game username and a clear explanation of your situation. See the Punishments page for full step-by-step instructions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use mods or texture packs?">
    Many mods are perfectly fine to use:

    - ✅ **Allowed:** Performance mods (Optifine, Sodium), minimaps without player radar, aesthetic resource packs, and HUD mods.
    - ❌ **Banned:** Hacked clients, X-ray texture packs or mods, and any mod that gives an unfair gameplay advantage over other players.

    When in doubt, ask in **#help-and-support** before using a mod.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Gameplay

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I claim land?">
    Hold a **Golden Shovel** and right-click two opposite corners of the area you want to protect. This creates a claim that prevents other players from building or breaking blocks inside it. See the Claims guide for full instructions and tips.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I earn money?">
    There are several ways to build your balance on The Grind MC:

    - Sell items at `/warp shop`
    - Join jobs with `/jobs join` and earn money while you play
    - Vote for the server with `/vote` to receive vote rewards and cash
    - Trade items with other players
    - List items on the auction house with `/ah`

    See the Economy page for a full breakdown of each method.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I die?">
    In non-PvP areas, your items drop at the location where you died. Use `/back` to quickly return to your death location and retrieve them before they despawn. In designated PvP zones, other players are able to pick up your dropped items, so enter at your own risk.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a world reset?">
    The main Survival world does **not** reset — your builds and progress are permanent. Resource worlds used for mining reset periodically to keep resources fresh for all players. Check **#announcements** on Discord for upcoming reset schedules.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Ranks & Donations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are the benefits of donor ranks?">
    Donor ranks come with a range of perks including extra home slots, bonus claim blocks, cosmetic particle trails, colored chat names, and more. See the Donor Ranks page for a full side-by-side comparison of every tier.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do donor ranks give pay-to-win advantages?">
    No. Every donor perk is either cosmetic or a quality-of-life convenience. Donor ranks do not provide gear, resources, or any advantage in combat — all players compete on an equal footing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long do donor ranks last?">
    Donor ranks are **permanent** and never expire. Once you've purchased a rank, it's yours to keep for as long as the server is running.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I purchased a rank but it has not applied — what do I do?">
    First, try fully logging out and back in to the server. If your rank still hasn't applied after relogging, contact staff in the Discord and provide your Minecraft username along with your receipt or transaction ID so they can look into it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I cannot connect to the server — what should I check?">
    Run through the following checklist:

    1. Confirm you're using the correct IP: `play.thegrindmc.net`
    2. Make sure you're on **Java Edition** (Bedrock is not supported)
    3. Check that your Minecraft version matches the supported version
    4. Check **#server-updates** on Discord for any ongoing maintenance or downtime
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I am experiencing high lag — what can I do?">
    Try these steps to improve your performance:

    - Reduce your render distance in Video Settings
    - Allocate more RAM to Minecraft via your launcher settings
    - Install a performance mod like Optifine or Sodium

    If the lag appears to be affecting everyone on the server rather than just you, report it in **#bug-reports** on Discord so staff are aware.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My items or progress disappeared — can staff restore them?">
    Staff are **not able** to restore items lost through natural gameplay causes such as death, fire, or falling. If you believe items disappeared due to a server bug rather than normal gameplay, report it in **#bug-reports** on Discord and include screenshots or any other evidence you have — the team will investigate.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
