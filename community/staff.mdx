The Grind MC is kept running fairly and smoothly by a dedicated team of staff volunteers who give their time to moderate the community, support players, and uphold the server rules. Understanding who does what — and how to reach the right person — means you'll always get help as quickly as possible.

## Staff Roles

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Owner" icon="crown">
    Oversees the entire server and makes the final call on all major decisions, from server direction to serious player disputes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Admin" icon="shield-halved">
    Manages the staff team day-to-day, handles the most serious issues, and has the authority to issue permanent bans.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Moderator" icon="gavel">
    Enforces the server rules across both the game and Discord, reviews player reports, and issues punishments where appropriate.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Helper" icon="hand">
    The first point of contact for new players — Helpers answer questions, guide players through features, and escalate anything serious to Moderators.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Contact Staff

You can reach the team through several channels depending on the situation:

- **In-game reports:** Use `/report [player] [reason]` to flag a rule-breaker directly to online staff.
- **General help in-game:** Type your question in chat or use `/msg [staffname]` to message a staff member directly if one is online.
- **On Discord:** Post your question in **#help-and-support** or send a direct message to an online staff member.
- **Urgent issues (e.g. ongoing griefing):** Ping **@Moderator** in Discord so available staff are alerted immediately.

## A Few Things to Keep in Mind

To save you time and set the right expectations, here are some situations where staff unfortunately aren't able to step in — not because they don't want to, but because of the limits of what's fair and technically possible:

<Info>
  - **Items lost to natural in-game causes** — deaths from falling, drowning, fire, or mobs are part of survival gameplay and can't be reversed.
  - **Refunds for player-to-player trades** — all deals made at player shops or through direct trades are considered final.
  - **Teleporting to arbitrary locations** — use `/warp` and `/home` commands for your travel needs.
  - **Acting as a middleman for player deals** — for safety and fairness, staff don't hold or transfer items on players' behalf.
</Info>

## Reporting a Rule Violation

If you witness someone breaking the rules, follow these steps to make sure your report is handled effectively:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Gather evidence">
    Take screenshots or a screen recording and note the coordinates of where the incident occurred. The more evidence you have, the faster staff can act.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the in-game report command">
    Run `/report [player] [reason]` to send an immediate alert to all online staff members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post on Discord if needed">
    If no staff are online or the issue is ongoing, post in **#player-reports** on Discord and attach your evidence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for staff to investigate">
    Staff will review the evidence and take appropriate action. Avoid confronting the reported player yourself.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive the outcome">
    If your report is confirmed, you will be notified of the action taken. All outcomes are handled at staff discretion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Applying for Staff

The Grind MC occasionally opens applications for new staff members. Here's what you need to know:

- **Applications are not always open.** Watch **#announcements** on Discord to find out when the next round of applications begins.
- **Meet the requirements.** Successful applicants typically have a good standing on the server, a reasonable amount of playtime, and demonstrate maturity and helpfulness in the community.
- **Your reputation matters.** Being active, helpful, and following the rules consistently is the best thing you can do to strengthen your application.

<Info>
  Asking staff to make you staff will NOT improve your chances. It may actually hurt them.
</Info>
