Discord is the main hub for The Grind MC community outside of the game. Whether you want to stay on top of the latest server news, get help from staff, appeal a punishment, or just hang out with fellow players, the Discord server is where it all happens. Joining is free and takes less than a minute.

## How to Join

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the invite link">
    Head to [discord.gg/thegrindmc](https://discord.gg/thegrindmc) to open the server invite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Discord account">
    If you don't already have a Discord account, sign up for free at [discord.com](https://discord.com) before accepting the invite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept the server rules">
    Once you've joined, read and accept the rules posted in **#rules** to gain access to the rest of the server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync your in-game rank">
    Run `/discord` in-game and follow the on-screen instructions to link your Minecraft account and unlock your Discord roles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Discord Rules

To keep the server a welcoming place for everyone, all members are expected to follow these rules:

- **Follow all main server rules.** The same standards that apply in-game apply here on Discord.
- **No advertising.** Do not promote other Minecraft servers or Discord servers in any channel.
- **Use the right channels.** Keep your conversations on-topic and in the appropriate channel.
- **No spam or excessive pinging.** Don't flood chat or repeatedly ping roles and members unnecessarily.
- **Respect all members.** Treat everyone — players and staff alike — with courtesy and respect.

<Note>
  Linking your Discord account to your in-game account unlocks special Discord roles and perks.
</Note>
