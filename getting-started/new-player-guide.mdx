Starting fresh on a new server can be a little overwhelming, but The Grind MC is designed to get you up and running quickly. Whether you're a seasoned Minecraft veteran or picking up a pickaxe for the first time, this guide covers everything you need to know about your first hours — from spawning in and grabbing your starter kit to claiming land, setting homes, and earning your first coins. Let's get you grinding.

## Spawning In

When you join The Grind MC for the first time, you'll land at **Spawn** — the server's central hub. Take a moment to look around:

- **The Welcome Board** is posted right at spawn. It gives you a quick overview of the server's key features, rules, and important commands. Give it a read before heading out.
- **Your Starter Kit** is waiting for you. Run `/kit starter` to receive a set of basic tools, food, and supplies to give you a fighting chance in the wild.
- **The Spawn Area** also contains portals and signs pointing you toward key warps like the shop, PvP zones, and more. Get familiar with your surroundings before venturing out.

## Your First Hour

The first hour on any survival server sets the tone for everything that comes after. Follow these steps to get off to a strong start.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Collect Your Starter Kit">
    Run `/kit starter` in chat to receive your new player kit. It includes basic tools, food, and a few helpful items to get you started without having to punch trees empty-handed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Travel Away From Spawn">
    The land around spawn is heavily trafficked and most of it is already claimed. Walk — or use `/rtp` if available — to travel a good distance out and find open, unclaimed wilderness to call your own.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gather Resources to Survive">
    Before settling down, collect the basics: **wood, stone, food, and coal**. Build a temporary shelter to survive your first night and keep your starter gear safe while you scout a permanent location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim Your First Plot with a Golden Shovel">
    Once you've found a spot you like, protect it immediately. Hold a **Golden Shovel**, right-click one corner of the area you want to claim, then right-click the opposite corner to define your claim. Any land inside that boundary is protected — only you (and anyone you trust) can build or break there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a Home with /sethome">
    Never lose your base again. Stand inside your claim and run `/sethome home` to save your location. You can teleport back at any time with `/home home`. You can set multiple named homes as you explore further.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Earning Money at /warp shop">
    The server economy runs on in-game currency. Head to `/warp shop` to sell the resources you've gathered — things like wood, stone, crops, and ores all have value. Use your earnings to buy better gear, expand your claim, or trade with other players.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Survival Tips

A few hard-earned tips to help you avoid the classic new-player mistakes.

<Tip>
  **Claim early, claim often.** Unclaimed land has no protection. Even if you've just placed a single chest, claim the area around it right away to prevent other players from accessing your items.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Don't build too close to spawn.** The area around spawn is busy and most land is already claimed or too contested. The further out you go, the more freedom you'll have to build without interruption.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Sell what you gather, not just what you craft.** Raw resources like logs, cobblestone, and crops sell consistently at `/warp shop`. You don't need to grind rare materials to start building up your balance.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Set multiple homes.** You're allowed to set more than one home. Use this to your advantage — save one at your base, one near a mine, and one close to spawn for easy access to the shop and warp hubs.
</Tip>

## Important Commands for New Players

Here are the essential commands you'll use every day on The Grind MC.

| Command | Description |
|---|---|
| `/kit starter` | Claim your new player starter kit |
| `/sethome [name]` | Save your current location as a named home |
| `/home [name]` | Teleport to a saved home |
| `/warp shop` | Visit the server shop to buy and sell items |
| `/balance` | Check your current in-game balance |
| `/rules` | Read the full server rules in-game |
