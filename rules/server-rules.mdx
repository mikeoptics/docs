The Grind MC is built on a foundation of respect, fairness, and fun. These rules exist to protect every player's experience — whether you're a first-day newcomer or a long-time veteran. Breaking the rules can result in punishment ranging from a warning to a permanent ban, so take the time to read and understand them fully.

<Warning>
  Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse. All players are expected to know and follow these rules from the moment they join the server.
</Warning>

## General Conduct

A healthy community starts with how you treat the people around you. You are expected to behave respectfully at all times.

1. Respect all players and staff — harassment, hate speech, and discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
2. Do not use offensive usernames, skins, or build offensive structures anywhere on the server.
3. Do not spam or flood the chat — this includes repeated messages, symbols, or nonsensical text.
4. Do not advertise other Minecraft servers or communities in any chat channel.
5. Keep discussions civil — excessive arguing, public drama, or attempts to stir conflict will be actioned.
6. Use English in global chat so staff can moderate effectively. Other channels may have different language rules.

## Griefing & Theft

Protecting the work of every player is a core part of The Grind MC experience.

- Griefing is strictly prohibited on both **claimed and unclaimed land**. Destroying, altering, or defacing another player's builds without permission is never acceptable.
- Do not steal from other players' chests, farms, storage systems, or builds under any circumstances.
- Raiding and PvP looting are **only permitted in designated PvP zones**. Outside of those areas, all players and their property are protected.

## Hacking & Cheating

All players must compete on a level playing field. Using software or tools that give you an unfair advantage ruins the experience for everyone.

**Allowed modifications:**
- Optifine and other visual enhancement mods
- Minimaps (without player radar / entity tracking features)
- Performance-improving mods (e.g. Sodium, Lithium)

**Prohibited modifications:**
- X-ray texture packs or mods
- Kill aura or combat hacks
- Fly hacks or movement exploits
- Auto-clickers or macro tools that automate gameplay
- Speed hacks or any form of movement manipulation

<Warning>
  Using any prohibited modification or client will result in a **permanent ban** with no appeal eligibility. If you are unsure whether a mod is allowed, ask a staff member before using it.
</Warning>

## Exploits & Bugs

Bugs and glitches happen — how you respond to them is what matters.

- Do not exploit bugs, glitches, or unintended game mechanics to gain an unfair advantage over other players or the economy.
- If you discover a bug or exploit, report it to staff immediately via Discord or a support ticket. Players who responsibly report exploits will be recognized and rewarded.

## Chat Rules

The chat is a shared space for the whole community. Keep it clean and enjoyable for all players.

- Do not use excessive caps — the occasional emphasis is fine, but all-caps messages will be considered spam.
- No NSFW, sexual, or otherwise mature content in any chat channel.
- Do not dox or share any player's personal information — real names, addresses, social media profiles, or any other private data. Violations of this rule are treated with the utmost severity.

## Building Rules

Your builds are an expression of your creativity — make sure they contribute positively to the server's world.

- Do not construct inappropriate, offensive, or hateful builds in any area of the server.
- Do not build within **100 blocks** of another player's claim without their explicit permission. Respect their space.
- Lag machines, chunk loaders that harm server performance, and any other intentional builds designed to destabilize or disrupt the server are strictly forbidden.

<Note>
  Staff have the final say in all rule interpretations and enforcement decisions. If you believe a punishment was unfair or incorrectly applied, you can submit an appeal in our Discord server.
</Note>
