The Grind MC uses a fair, graduated punishment system designed to correct behavior before escalating to more serious consequences. Minor offenses are handled with warnings and short mutes, while severe or repeated violations can result in a permanent ban. Staff exercise judgment on a case-by-case basis, and all punishments are logged for transparency and consistency.

## Punishment Tiers

Each punishment tier is matched to the severity of the offense. Staff will always try to use the appropriate level — but repeat offenses and serious violations will accelerate the escalation.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="⚠️ Warning" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Issued for minor first offenses and low-level chat violations. A warning is a formal notice that your behavior needs to change. Accumulating multiple warnings leads to stricter action.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🔇 Mute" icon="microphone-slash">
    Applied for repeated chat violations, spam, or harassment. A mute prevents you from sending messages in chat for a set duration. The length increases with each offense.
  </Card>
  <Card title="👢 Kick" icon="right-from-bracket">
    Used for disruptive behavior that requires immediate enforcement. A kick removes you from the server but allows you to reconnect. It serves as a strong warning before a ban is issued.
  </Card>
  <Card title="⏳ Temporary Ban" icon="clock">
    Issued for serious rule violations. Duration varies based on the severity and history of the offense, ranging from a few hours to several weeks. You may appeal a temporary ban through our Discord.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🚫 Permanent Ban" icon="ban">
    Reserved for hacking, severe griefing, or players who continue to violate rules after a temporary ban. Permanent bans are not lifted unless a successful appeal is submitted and approved by senior staff.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Common Violations & Expected Punishments

The table below outlines the typical punishments for common rule violations. Staff may deviate from these guidelines when the situation warrants it.

| Violation                     | First Offense              | Repeat Offense             |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Chat spam                     | Warning / Short mute       | Extended mute              |
| Mild harassment               | Warning                    | Temporary ban              |
| Griefing unclaimed land       | Warning + rollback         | Temporary ban              |
| Griefing claimed land         | Temporary ban + rollback   | Permanent ban              |
| Hacking / cheating            | Permanent ban              | N/A                        |
| Advertising other servers     | Permanent ban              | N/A                        |

## Appealing a Punishment

If you believe a punishment was applied in error or was disproportionate to your offense, you have the right to appeal. Follow the steps below to submit your appeal properly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the Discord Server">
    If you are not already a member, join The Grind MC official Discord server. The invite link is available on our website and server listing pages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to the #appeals Channel">
    Find the **#appeals** channel in the Discord server. Read any pinned messages before proceeding — they contain important instructions and the current appeal template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Your Appeal">
    Create a new appeal post using the provided form or template. Be honest, concise, and respectful. Include your in-game username, the type of punishment, the date it was issued, and your reason for appealing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for a Staff Review">
    A staff member will review your appeal, typically within **48 hours**. During this time, do not contact staff members individually to ask about your appeal status — this may delay the process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Await the Decision">
    Staff will respond to your appeal with a decision. If your appeal is approved, your punishment will be reduced or removed. If it is denied, the original punishment stands.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Ban evasion is a serious offense.** If you create or use an alternate account to play on The Grind MC while your main account is banned, **all associated accounts will be permanently banned** with no appeal eligibility. Wait out your punishment or go through the proper appeal process.
</Info>

## Staff Abuse Reports

Staff members are held to the highest standard of conduct on The Grind MC. If you witness or experience a staff member abusing their powers, acting unprofessionally, or behaving in a manner that violates our community values, you have the right to report it.

To report a staff member, send a direct message to a **Senior Staff member or Administrator** in our Discord server. Include as much evidence as possible — screenshots, screen recordings, and timestamps help ensure your report is taken seriously and investigated promptly. All reports are treated confidentially.
