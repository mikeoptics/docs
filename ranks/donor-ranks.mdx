## How to Purchase

Purchasing your donor rank takes just a few minutes. Follow the steps below and your rank will be active on the server automatically.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the server store">
    Head to [store.thegrindmc.net](https://store.thegrindmc.net) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your rank">
    Browse the available donor rank tiers and click on the one you'd like to purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your Minecraft username">
    Type in your **Java Edition** Minecraft username exactly as it appears in-game. Double-check the spelling before proceeding!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Choose your preferred payment method and complete the checkout process securely.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in or relog on the server">
    Join or rejoin The Grind MC — your donor rank will be applied to your account automatically. No need to contact staff.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All purchases are final and non-refundable. If you experience any issues with your rank not being applied, please contact a staff member on Discord and we'll get it sorted out for you.
</Note>

## Rank Upgrades

Already a donor and want to level up? You can upgrade from a lower donor rank to a higher one at any time through the store. Upgrade discounts may be available — check the store for current pricing before you buy.

## Gifting Ranks

Want to treat a friend? Donor ranks can be gifted to any other player directly through the store. Simply enter **their Minecraft username** in the username field during checkout instead of your own, and the rank will be applied to their account automatically.

<Info>
  Donor ranks are **permanent and never expire**. Once you purchase a rank, it's yours for life on The Grind MC. Thank you so much for supporting the server — it truly makes a difference!
</Info>

<Warning>
  Never purchase ranks from third parties or other players. Only buy from the official store at **store.thegrindmc.net** to guarantee your rank is delivered correctly and your payment is secure.
</Warning>
