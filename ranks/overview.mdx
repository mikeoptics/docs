The Grind MC features two types of ranks: **free play ranks** that you earn simply by playing on the server, and **donor ranks** that you can purchase to support the server and unlock exclusive perks. Whether you're grinding your way up the ladder or looking to show your support, there's a rank for everyone.

## Free Ranks

Free ranks are earned through playtime and in-game achievements — no purchase required. The more you play, the more you unlock!

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Default" icon="user">
    The starting rank for all new players. You get basic permissions to explore, build, and join the community right away.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Member" icon="star">
    Earned after **5 hours** of playtime. Unlocks extra home slots and the ability to use colors in chat.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Veteran" icon="shield">
    Earned after **50 hours** of playtime. Grants more claim blocks, access to `/nick` for a custom nickname, and cosmetic particle effects.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Elite" icon="crown">
    Earned after **200 hours** of playtime. Includes all Veteran perks plus exclusive kits and priority queue access so you never wait to join.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

To check your current rank progress or advance to the next tier, use `/rankup` or `/rank` in-game at any time.

## Donor Ranks

Donor ranks are available for purchase in the server store and are a fantastic way to support The Grind MC while unlocking some great extras. There are three tiers — **Grinder**, **Champion**, and **Legend** — each with its own set of perks.

Head over to the [Donor Ranks](/ranks/donor-ranks) page for the full breakdown of perks, pricing, and how to purchase.

<Note>
  Donor ranks support the server and unlock cosmetic and convenience perks — but they do **not** give pay-to-win advantages in PvP or survival competition. The playing field stays fair for everyone.
</Note>

## How Ranks Stack

Free ranks and donor ranks are designed to work together. Your donor rank perks are **additive** to whatever free rank you currently hold — so as you keep playing and climbing the free rank ladder, your donor perks continue to stack on top. The higher you climb in both systems, the more you unlock!
