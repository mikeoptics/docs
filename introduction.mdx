## Getting Started in 4 Steps

New to The Grind MC? Here's all you need to get up and running fast.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to the Server">
    Launch Minecraft Java Edition, head to **Multiplayer**, and add our server IP: `play.thegrindmc.net`. Hit connect and you'll spawn right in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the Rules">
    Before you start building or battling, take a moment to read the server rules. You can find them posted at spawn or by running `/rules` in chat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim Your Land">
    Protect your builds and resources by claiming your own plot of land using a **Golden Shovel**. No claim means no protection — so stake your territory early.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the Discord">
    Connect with the community, get the latest server news, open a support ticket, or just hang out. Our Discord is the hub for everything happening on The Grind MC.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Makes The Grind MC Unique

The Grind MC isn't just another survival server — it's a full experience built around the things that make Minecraft worth playing.

- **Player Economy** — Buy, sell, and trade with other players through a living, breathing in-game economy. Head to `/warp shop` to get started, or set up your own player shop.
- **Land Claims** — Your builds are yours. The Grind MC uses a robust land claim system so griefers can never touch what you've worked hard to create.
- **PvP Zones** — Want a fight? Dedicated PvP zones let you test your skills against other players without putting your base at risk.
- **Active Community** — From seasonal events to player-run markets, there's always something happening on The Grind MC. A great server is nothing without great players — and we've got plenty of them.
