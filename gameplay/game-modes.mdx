## Survival

Survival is the beating heart of The Grind MC. It's where most players spend their time, and for good reason — there's always something to do, someone to trade with, or a new area to explore.

**World & Biomes**

The Survival world is massive, featuring all vanilla biomes plus custom terrain generation. The world does **not** reset, so every build you make and every block you place is here to stay. You can explore as far as you dare — the further from spawn you venture, the more untouched terrain you'll find.

**Economy Integration**

Everything in Survival ties into the server-wide economy. Mine ores, sell them at `/warp shop`, complete jobs, and build your wealth over time. The economy is entirely player-driven, so your actions directly shape the server's market.

**Land Claiming**

Your builds are protected through our land claiming system. Using a Golden Shovel, you can claim chunks of land that only you (and players you trust) can modify. No more worrying about griefers — what you build is yours. Check out the [Claims guide](/gameplay/claims) for the full rundown.

**PvP Zones**

PvP isn't forced on anyone in Survival. The main world is peaceful, but venture beyond the wilderness boundary or step into designated PvP arenas if you're looking for a fight. Rewards await those bold enough to take the risk.

**Custom Mob Drops & Enchantments**

Survival on The Grind MC goes beyond vanilla. Mobs have a chance to drop custom loot, and you'll find custom enchantments on gear that can't be found anywhere else. Keep an eye on those drops — some are incredibly rare and highly sought-after on the auction house.

## Skyblock

Think you can build an empire from a single tree and a handful of dirt? Skyblock puts that to the test. You start on a tiny floating island in the void and work your way up to a thriving sky kingdom.

**Your Starting Island**

When you join Skyblock for the first time, you'll receive a starter island with a tree, a chest of basic supplies, and a small patch of dirt. From there, it's up to you. Expand your island by completing challenges, earning island points, and gathering resources.

**Island Challenges & Rewards**

Skyblock is packed with challenges that reward you for hitting milestones — crafting your first tools, growing your first crops, or reaching a certain island level. Completing challenges earns you coins, rare items, and island upgrades that help you progress faster.

**Leaderboards**

Think your island is the best on the server? Prove it. Skyblock leaderboards rank players by island level, challenge completions, and wealth. Compete for top spots to earn recognition and exclusive prizes.

**Getting Started**

Ready to jump in? Just type `/warp skyblock` from anywhere on the server to teleport to the Skyblock hub and start your island adventure.

## Switching Game Modes

Moving between game modes is quick and easy. You have two options:

- **Warp Commands** — Type `/warp [destination]` to teleport directly to a game mode hub. For example, `/warp skyblock` takes you straight to Skyblock.
- **Compass Menu** — Right-click your compass at any time to open the navigation menu. From here, you can click any game mode icon to teleport instantly — no commands needed.

Your inventory and progress are saved separately for each game mode, so you won't accidentally mix up your Survival gear with your Skyblock items.

<Tip>
  **New to The Grind MC?** Start with **Survival** mode. It's the most feature-rich experience on the server and gives you the best introduction to our economy, community, and gameplay systems. Once you've got your footing, branching into Skyblock or the Mini-Games is a great way to keep things fresh!
</Tip>
