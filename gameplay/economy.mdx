The Grind MC runs on a fully player-driven economy — and that means **you** are in control. Prices fluctuate, markets shift, and smart players can turn a humble mining session into serious wealth. Whether you're a merchant running a player shop empire, a job specialist grinding levels, or just someone who wants to fund their next big build, understanding the economy is key to thriving on the server.

## Currency

The server's currency is **coins**. Every player starts with a small amount to get on their feet, and from there it's all about hustle. Coins are used for everything — buying items, paying other players, upgrading your claim, and more. You can check your balance at any time with `/balance` (or `/bal` for short).

Coins are earned through multiple methods, so there's always a way to make money no matter your playstyle.

## Earning Money

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sell Items at the Server Shop">
    The server shop at `/warp shop` buys and sells a wide range of items at fixed prices. Gather resources — ores, crops, mob drops — and sell them directly to the shop for instant coins. It's the most straightforward way to earn, especially early on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trade with Other Players">
    Player-to-player trading is where the real economy happens. Use `/trade [player]` to open a secure trade window with another player, or set up a player shop so others can buy from you even when you're offline. Supply and demand rule here — if something's rare, you can name your price.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Jobs">
    The Jobs plugin lets you specialize in a role and get paid for doing what you already love. Join a job with `/jobs join` and earn coins every time you perform actions related to that job. The more you do it, the higher your job level climbs — and higher levels mean better pay rates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Vote for the Server">
    Voting is quick, free, and rewarding. Type `/vote` to get a list of voting sites. Each vote earns you coins plus bonus rewards like crate keys. You can vote once per site per day, so make it part of your daily routine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Quests and Challenges">
    The server is full of quests and challenges that reward coins on completion. Check your quest log regularly — some quests are time-limited and offer the best coin-per-hour rates on the server.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Spending Money

Once your pockets are full, here's how to put those coins to work:

- **Server Shop** — Head to `/warp shop` to buy items at fixed prices. Great for hard-to-find materials.
- **Player Shops** — Browse player-owned shops scattered across the server for deals that often beat the server shop prices.
- **Auction House** — Use `/ah` to browse items listed by other players. You can snag rare gear, bulk materials, and exclusive items here.
- **Claim Upgrades & Perks** — Spend coins to expand your claim blocks, unlock perks, or access premium areas of the server.

## Player Shops

Player shops are one of the best parts of The Grind MC's economy. You can set up your own shop in your claim and sell items to other players automatically — even while you're offline.

**Setting Up a Chest Shop**

1. Place a chest in your claim where you'd like your shop to be.
2. Hold the item you want to sell (or leave it in the chest).
3. Place a sign on the side of the chest with the following format:

| Line | Content |
|------|---------|
| Line 1 | Your player name (or leave blank — it fills automatically) |
| Line 2 | Quantity per transaction (e.g. `10`) |
| Line 3 | Buy price : Sell price (e.g. `5 : 3`) |
| Line 4 | Item name (e.g. `Diamond`) |

4. The sign will confirm your shop is active. Stock the chest with items and you're open for business!

<Note>
  Player shops can be set up anywhere inside your own claim. Make sure your shop is fully within your claimed land — shops placed outside a claim are not protected and could be tampered with. Keep your chest stocked so customers are never turned away!
</Note>

## Auction House

The Auction House is the server's global marketplace — a one-stop shop for buying and selling items with the entire server community.

- **Browse listings** — Type `/ah` to open the Auction House and scroll through everything currently for sale.
- **List an item** — Hold the item you want to sell in your hand and type `/ah sell [price]` to list it instantly.
- **Item expiry** — Unsold items are automatically returned to you after **48 hours**, so re-list anything that doesn't sell.
- **Collect earnings** — When your item sells, your coins are deposited automatically. Check `/ah` to collect any expired items.

<Info>
  The Auction House is the best place to sell rare drops, custom enchanted gear, and bulk resources. Browse it often — great deals appear and disappear fast!
</Info>

## Jobs

Jobs are the backbone of steady income on The Grind MC. Pick a role that matches how you like to play, and you'll earn coins naturally just by doing your thing.

**Joining a Job**

Type `/jobs join [jobname]` to join a job. You can be a member of multiple jobs at once, though your earnings may be split between them.

**Available Jobs**

| Job | What You Do |
|-----|------------|
| Miner | Mine ores and stone |
| Farmer | Grow and harvest crops |
| Woodcutter | Chop down trees |
| Hunter | Kill mobs and animals |
| Builder | Place blocks and build structures |
| Fisherman | Catch fish and treasure |
| Crafter | Craft items and recipes |

**Leveling Up**

Every action you perform in your job earns experience toward your job level. As your level increases, your pay rate per action increases too — so the longer you stick with a job, the more rewarding it becomes. Dedicated job players can earn significantly more than newcomers, so it pays to commit.

Type `/jobs stats` to check your current level and earnings breakdown.
